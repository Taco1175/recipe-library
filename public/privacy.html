The short version: We collect only what's necessary to run your recipe library. We don't sell your data, we don't show you ads, and you can delete your account at any time.

1. Who We Are

Recipe Library ("we", "us", "our") is a personal meal planning and recipe management application. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our service at cowpushing-meals.netlify.app.

2. Information We Collect

3. How We Use Your Information

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the app.

4. Google Calendar Integration

If you choose to connect Google Calendar, we request permission to read your calendar events and create new meal plan events. This access is used solely to display your schedule and export meal plans. We do not store your calendar data on our servers ‚Äî it is fetched in real time and displayed only to you.

5. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored in Supabase, a secure cloud database provider. All data is encrypted at rest and in transit using TLS. Row-level security policies ensure that your recipes are only accessible to you (and anyone you explicitly share them with).

Passwords are hashed using industry-standard algorithms and are never stored or transmitted in plain text.

7. Your Rights

To delete your account or request a data export, contact us using the details below.

8. Cookies & Local Storage

We use browser localStorage to store your authentication session token and app preferences (such as your selected view mode and meal plan). We do not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics cookies.

9. Children's Privacy
